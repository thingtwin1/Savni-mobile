All about Savni!!!

When I was young, I participated in many activities. I did ballet for 1 year, but then I quit. I went to swimming classes, but then I moved. I joined student council and scrapbooking club in middle school, but those clubs were not there in high school. I joined dance again, but then I twisted my knee so I stopped.
